# 🧪 User Acceptance Testing Guide

!!! info "You're helping shape Memex"
    This guide is for invited testers. There's no wrong answer — your honest impressions are exactly what we need. Nothing you do here will break anything.

**Access Memex at:** [memex.shivelymedia.com](https://memex.shivelymedia.com) — sign in when prompted.

**Please test on your phone first, then desktop if you have time.**

Estimated time: **15–20 minutes**

---

## 1. First Impressions

Open the app on your phone and just look around for a moment.

- [ ] Does it load cleanly?
- [ ] Does anything look broken or cut off?
- [ ] Does the layout feel natural on a phone? (Navigation at the bottom, content readable)
- [ ] Try rotating your phone to landscape — does anything look wrong?

---

## 2. Starting a Conversation

Tap the **Chat** tab at the bottom of the screen.

- [ ] Type any question you're genuinely curious about and send it
- [ ] Does the response stream in smoothly, or does it feel choppy?
- [ ] Is the text easy to read?
- [ ] Ask a follow-up question in the same conversation

!!! tip "Try these if you're not sure what to ask"
    - "What's a good way to learn guitar as an adult?"
    - "Explain how a black hole forms in simple terms"
    - "Write me a short poem about coffee"

---

## 3. The Keyboard *(phone only)*

- [ ] Tap the message box — does the keyboard appear without covering the send button?
- [ ] Does the **Send button on the keyboard** (arrow icon) work to send your message?
- [ ] Type a `/` character — does a small command menu appear above the keyboard?

---

## 4. Chat History

Start 2–3 conversations on different topics.

- [ ] Tap the **☰ menu icon** (bottom right) — does a sidebar slide in from the left?
- [ ] Can you see your past conversations listed?
- [ ] Can you tap a past conversation and switch to it?
- [ ] Does it show the right content when you switch?

---

## 5. Themes

In the sidebar (tap **☰**), scroll to the very bottom.

- [ ] Find the **Theme** selector and try a different theme
- [ ] Does the whole app change appearance?
- [ ] Does your theme choice stick if you close and reopen the app?

---

## 6. Navigation

Tap each tab at the bottom of the screen:

| Tab | What to check |
|-----|---------------|
| **Chat** | Opens chat, can start new conversations |
| **Art** | Art Studio section loads |
| **Tools** | Tools section loads |
| **Settings** | Can see your account name and a sign-out button |

- [ ] Does each section load without errors?
- [ ] Does the back button / navigation feel natural?

---

## 7. Desktop Check *(if you have time)*

Open [memex.shivelymedia.com](https://memex.shivelymedia.com) on a laptop or desktop.

- [ ] Does the sidebar appear on the left?
- [ ] Is the layout noticeably different from mobile? (It should be)
- [ ] Does everything that worked on mobile also work on desktop?

---

## Your Feedback

Please share your answers to these questions — even a few words per item is helpful:

**1. What device and browser did you use?**
*(e.g. "iPhone 15, Safari" or "Android, Chrome")*

**2. Did anything look visually wrong or misaligned?**

**3. Did anything not work as you expected?**

**4. Was anything confusing or unclear?**

**5. One thing you liked:**

**6. One thing you would change:**

**7. Overall first impression (1–5):**
*(1 = very rough, 5 = polished and usable)*

---

!!! success "Thank you"
    Your feedback directly influences what gets improved next. There are no wrong answers — if something felt off, that's the most useful thing you can tell us.
